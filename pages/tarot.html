Why this deck

Tarot built on the wheel.

Most tarot decks treat astrology as a footnote. This one starts there.

Every card has an astrological assignment that earned its place — The Magician is Mercury; The Empress is Venus; the Two of Wands opens the first decan of Aries. When you lay a spread, you're laying a chart. When you read your chart, you can lay it.

The art is hand-painted, then printed on heavy linen-finish stock. The deck ships with a guidebook keying every card to its decan, dignity, and traditional rulership.
