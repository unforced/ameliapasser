Arcana in the Seven Spheres presents a ritually constructed system developed over four years through astrological timing, planetary invocation, and material correspondence — exhibited here for the first time as a unified body, in its first shared physical space.

Each work was initiated under precise celestial conditions, aligned with planetary dignities, days, and hours, and produced through invocations of planetary intelligences informed by sources such as the Picatrix and the Orphic Hymns. Pigments and substrates were selected for their traditional associations: lapis lazuli for Jupiter, iron oxide for Mars, gold and silver leaf for the luminaries.

The Major Arcana emerged through this process, followed by the elemental works aligned to the solstices and equinoxes — structured in dialogue with the Kabbalistic Tree of Life. Together, these works initiate an environment in which image, ritual, and cosmos converge.

This is a rare opportunity to collect from this fully realized body of work. Eleven paintings, hybrid bidding.

The complete Seven Basics + Minor Arcana — eleven oils on canvas — will be auctioned as a hybrid in-person and online event. Each painting starts at a base price; highest bidder wins.

In-room collectors bid live throughout the evening; out-of-town collectors join online via a dedicated bidding platform (registration details published closer to the event). Proceeds support Amelia's graduate studies.
