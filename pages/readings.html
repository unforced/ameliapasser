Questions

Common questions

I don't know my exact birth time. Can we still do a natal?

Yes — I'll work with what you have, and tell you which parts of the reading are time-sensitive (rising sign, house cusps, lunar timing). For a date-only reading I'll often steer toward a noon chart and focus on the planets and their aspects.

Is this religious? Do I have to believe in astrology?

No, and no. Astrology and tarot are languages — symbol systems for naming what's already happening. You don't have to believe in them for them to be useful, any more than you have to believe in dreams to find meaning in them.

Can I record the session?

Natal sessions include a recording by default. For tarot sessions I'm happy to record on request — just let me know when we start.

Do you do predictions?

I work descriptively more than predictively. I can show you what the symbols are pointing toward and what your timing looks like, but I won't tell you what to do or guarantee outcomes. The future is yours.

What if I'm in a difficult moment?

Many people come for readings during hard times — that's part of what this work is for. I take it seriously. If something we discuss is outside what a reading can hold, I'll say so and point you toward more appropriate support.
